  • Genre: First-Person Shooter (FPS)

How to Play

  1. Start the Game:
    When you enter the game, you will be in a shooting scenario and need to fight against other enemies. The controls are simple and easy to learn.
  2. Controls:
    • Mouse: Move to aim, left-click to shoot.
    • Keyboard: W/A/S/D to move, Space to jump, Shift to sprint, Ctrl to crouch, R to reload, E to interact, Q to switch weapons.
    • Gamepad: Left stick to move, right stick to aim, buttons to shoot and interact.
  3. Game Interface:
    • Health (HP): Shown on screen, decreases when hit; zero means game over.
    • Ammo: Shows remaining ammo; press R to reload.
    • Weapon Switching: Press Q to switch weapons.
  4. Gameplay and Objectives:
    The main goal is to defeat all enemies and survive. Keep moving, dodge attacks, and shoot enemies. Enemies spawn at different map locations and require quick reactions. Use tactics and map control to gain advantage.
  5. Tips:
    • Use cover to avoid attacks.
    • Quickly switch weapons to handle different enemies.
    • Aim for the head for more damage.
    • Monitor ammo and reload or switch weapons in time.
  6. Modes and Settings:
    • Fullscreen mode: Click the fullscreen button in-game.
    • Settings: Adjust volume and graphics options.
  7. Note:
    • Built with HTML5 technology, runs smoothly on modern browsers with no downloads required.
    • Supports mobile devices with Android version available.
    • Includes a beginner tutorial to help new players get started.
